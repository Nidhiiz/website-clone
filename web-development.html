Frequently asked question
  • Q1.How long does it take to develop a website with WebCodian?

    The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity and specific requirements of your project. We'll work closely with you to understand your needs and provide an estimated timeline during the consultation phase.

  • Q2.Can you incorporate e-commerce functionality into my website?

    Yes, we have extensive experience in developing e-commerce websites with features such as product listings, shopping carts, secure payment gateways, and order management systems.

  • Q3. Will my website be mobile-responsive?

    Absolutely, all websites developed by WebCodian are optimized for mobile devices to ensure a seamless user experience across different screen sizes and devices.

    Yes, we offer maintenance packages to keep your website updated, secure, and running smoothly after launch. Our maintenance services include regular backups, security updates, content updates, and technical support.

    Certainly, we offer website redesign services to give your existing site a modern and visually appealing makeover. Whether you want to update the design, improve functionality, or enhance user experience, our team can help breathe new life into your website.
