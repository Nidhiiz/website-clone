Frequently asked question
  • Q1. What is included in your website maintenance packages?

    Our website maintenance packages include regular backups, security updates, content updates, bug fixes, performance optimization, and technical support to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and fully functional.

  • Q2. How often do you perform maintenance tasks on websites?

    The frequency of maintenance tasks depends on the specific needs of your website and your chosen maintenance package. We typically perform regular maintenance tasks on a monthly or quarterly basis, but can also provide custom maintenance schedules based on your requirements.

  • Q3. Do you offer emergency support for critical issues outside of regular maintenance hours?

    Yes, we understand that emergencies can occur outside of regular maintenance hours, and we provide emergency support services to address critical issues promptly and efficiently.

  • Q4. Can I customize my website maintenance package based on my needs?

    Yes, we offer flexible maintenance packages that can be customized to suit your specific needs and budget. Whether you require basic maintenance tasks or more comprehensive support, we can tailor a package that meets your requirements..

  • Q5. How do I request maintenance or support for my website?

    You can request maintenance or support for your website by contacting our support team via email, phone, or our online ticketing system. Our team will promptly address your request and provide assistance as needed.
